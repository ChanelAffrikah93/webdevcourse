Why study with us?

Danger!


Yeah, you could go to a normal school and feel all safe... Or
you could come here where we will actively throw you in to
life threatening situations!
We will ease you in gently, where you will enjoy the delights
of backfiring spells exploding in your face. From there we will
progress you to fighting an assortment of dangerous
creatures. You may even get the opportunity to risk your life
against huge dragons for sport! Cool as, right?
Even some of out staff are happy to concuss you with a book
if you don't look like you're studying.

Nature!


Do you like nature? Great! As you walk around our grounds
you will quickly learn that nature here doesn't like you!
Get up close and personal with our willow tree and discover
that you never truly get a day off fom life threatening
situations around here.
Do you like spiders? Great! We have GIANT hairy ones that you
can totally chat to before exeriencing the thrill of running
for your life.

The Chosen One Package!!!!!


If you need more, don't worry - we got you!
Upgrade to your chosed one package and we will provide you
with your very oen arch enemy who will stop at nothing to
eviscerate you!
You'll also have an amazibg time learning politics as your
enemy take control of the government and turns the whole
world against you!
Still not eneough? Well, if you hang on in there you will finally
get a boss battle where the stakes are legitamately high. How
cool is that?
